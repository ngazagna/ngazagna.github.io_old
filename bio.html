Nidham Gazagnadou - Short Bio & CVs

I finished my PhD on Stochastic Optimization methods for Machine Learning problems at Télécom Paris in December 2021.

Before that, I graduated from ENSTA Paris with a major in Applied Mathematics in 2018. In parallel of my third year at ENSTA I also graduated from the MVA Master (Mathematics, Computer Vision, Machine Learning) at ENS Cachan.
